Ye Olde Hurdy-Gurdy Museum of Vintage Radio is located in the Martello Tower and houses a fine collection of exhibits chronicling the history of telecommunications from the 1840s to date. There are many examples of early Morse equipment, gramophones, crystal sets, valve radios and other pieces of equipment. Pat Herbert, the curator is always happy to demonstrate some of the many working exhibits.
