Draiocht is an Arts Centre based in the heart of Blanchardstown. Since opening in 2001, Dra ocht has reached right to the heart of the people it serves in Dublin 15 and its environs to provide stimulating and accessible arts programming. Over 50,000 people a year attend performance events including a mix of professional, national, international and local work
