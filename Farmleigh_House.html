Farmleigh House is located on an estate of 78 acres to the north-west of Dublins Phoenix Park. It was originally a small Georgian house built in the late 18th century. In 1881-84 it was refurbished, extended to the west, and a third storey added. In 1896 the Ballroom wing was added, and in 1901 the Conservatory was added.
