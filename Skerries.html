Skerries Mills Industrial Heritage Centre comprises a watermill, 5 sail windmill, 4 sail windmill and associated mill races, mill pond and wetlands. The history of the mills can be traced back to the 16th Century and a bakery was established on the site by 1840. The mills are a rare survival of 17th., 18th. and 19th. century industrial history. The mills enclose a four-acre mill field where the annual crop is grown.
